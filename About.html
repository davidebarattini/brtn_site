About

I’m an Italian photographer and videomaker, passionate about capturing stories and emotions through images. My main focus is sports photography, where I can freeze the energy, movement, and intensity of every moment.

At the same time, I enjoy experimenting and exploring different styles, because I believe every project is a chance to grow and see things from new perspectives.

My goal is to turn fleeting moments into timeless visual memories. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Visual Design and I’m currently completing my studies in Visual Communication at SUPSI in Mendrisio.
