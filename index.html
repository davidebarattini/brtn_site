Projects

Garpez Racing - Avoriaz Bike Park

Two days riding in the heart of the French Alps at the Avoriaz bikepark. Capturing speed, style and the raw beauty of the mountains through every shot. A project born to share the passion for freeride and the energy of the community that lives for two wheels.

Garpez Racing - Madesimo Bike Park

Two days at Madesimo bikepark, trying out some shots and capturing the flow of the trails. A short project focused on experimenting and having fun on two wheels.

Studio Photography - Milan

Studio sessions in Milan focused on clean light, minimal sets and the presence of each model in front of the lens. This project explores expression, attitude and detail, building a visual narrative that blends fashion aesthetics with a contemporary studio approach.

Trofeo Villa d'Este - Como

Two days immersed in the timeless atmosphere of the Trofeo Villa d’Este ACI Como. Surrounded by iconic cars, refined details and the elegance of Lake Como, this project focuses on capturing the dialogue between heritage, design and motion. A visual story dedicated to the culture of classic automobiles and the unique charm of one of the most prestigious automotive events.

Constructed Views - Swiss

A one-week university project set in the abandoned Saceba cement factory, resulting in a black and white triptych. The work interprets the industrial space through composition, contrast and structured visual perspectives.
